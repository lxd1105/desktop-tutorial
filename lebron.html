The Career and Life of Lebron James

Lebron Raymone James, born on December 30, 1984, in Akron, Ohio, is one of the greatest basketball players in the sport of Basketball. Lebron has shown what it takes to be a dominant athlete in his sport.

Early Life and Career

Lebron rose to be a phenom at St. Vincent-St. Mary High School in Akron, where his skills put him on the map to be nationally televised and was dubbed “The Chosen One” by Sports Illustrated at the age of 17. Skipping college, he declared for the 2003 NBA Draft and was selected as the #1 overall pick by the Cleveland Cavaliers.

Lebron’s Franchise with Cleveland Cavaliers

He brought spirit and success to a struggling franchise, even bringing home a championship in 2016 after being there from 2003–2010, and 2014–2018. The championship ended the 52-year title drought in Cleveland.

Franchise Teams

  1. Cleveland Cavaliers (2003–2010, 2014–2018)
  2. Miami Heat (2010–2014)
  3. Los Angeles Lakers (2018–present)

Achievements

  • 4× NBA Champion
  • NBA’s all-time leading scorer
  • 4× NBA Most Valuable Player
